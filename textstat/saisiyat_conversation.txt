Qabowan :	saQiwaz ay? SoQo QampowaQ raHam?
tapaS :	siya saQsaQih ka hahilaH Qam maehrahrang ka lalekeH, sawsawaQan nanaw.
Qabowan :	nisiya kapaeQonhaelan minkoringan SayQinoQ?
tapaS :	kapaeQonhaelan minkoringan nisiya Say kilapaH.
Qabowan :	hinghaQ ay SaySiyat?
tapaS :	QokaQ! siya SaypapaHaS.
Qabowan :	nisiyaQ kapaeQonhaelan minkoringan kayzaeh ay kitaQen?
tapaS :	nisiya kapaeQonhaelan minkoringan kayzaeh kitaQen o makakreng.
Qabowan :	yaHo SararaQ hi siyaQ tomihoroeH ila Qima kayzaeh kapaeQonhaelan minkoringan.
---
ray tawQan
tapaS :	QaeQaew, niSoQ tinnon kaybaHen Qini hiya?
QaeQaew :	noka korkoring maQan kahibaHen.
tapaS :	siya Qam rimaQ homlal ay?
QaeQaew :	nasiya kakiSkaHatan Qam rimaQ QaQinolaQ ka homlal ki kapaHtol.
tapaS :	QaHay! siya raHam ay maHtol ki homlal?
QaeQaew :	raHam o! Qaring Qima QoelQoelaeQan tani tatiniQ rimaQ paStaQay. siya maQ raHam ila.
tapaS :	nimon kapaHtol ki homlal kayzaeh ay kalalohayen?
QaeQaew :	nom homlal kayzaeh kalalohayen, SoH rawak ila maQ homlal ila. kapaHtol Qaewhay kapaHtolen.
tapaS :	yaHo kayzaeh ay rimaQ komitaQ nasiya SiQaQinolaQ ?
QaeQaew :	QaHay! SoH potngor ila kaQaQinolaQan QitaQ tapaeQonhael pakilwaQis inasiya.
---
kapaStaQayan
taHin :	SaySiyat hayzaQ ay ka Qima nonak kaspengan?
kalih :	hayzaQ, ray noka motoH lampez QilaS, yami maQ paStaQay ila.
taHin :	kozaQ kinhonaehngeH monhael Qam paStaQay.
kalih :	roSaQ tinalQoemaeh monhael Qam paStaQay, lampez tinalQoemaeh monhael somaton.
taHin :	QampowaQ hayzaQ ka hini kaspengan paStaQay?
kalih :	yami makakSiyaeQ ki taQay, pawaHiQ hi taQay mowaHiQ lalohay kil yami.
taHin :	SoH Qam paStaQay ila, Qakoy ay katalQawanen howaw?
kalih :	Qakoy o, yami Qam paStaQay minSalaQ QaehaeQ QilaS, maQ talQawan, kakoSinraehoeQ tatilhaehael QiSka SinraehoeQ nonak hayzaQ kapatawawen.
taHin :	hayzaQ ay katiyaHawen Qima poSiyan?
kalih :	SoH paStaQay potngor ila SaySiyat maQ tomiyaHaw ila, Qokik paeQoeQoeQ Qokik tomayhobaz Qam paepaeQaehaeQ, nakhini kayzaeh niQ taQay maQ Qokik paSiQayoQ ani.
---
ray kakiSkaHatan
kama tortoroeQ :	SoQo raHam ay ray taywan hayzaQ pizaQ SinSinraehoeQ?
kama kiSkaHat :	hayzaQ lompez o Sopat SinSinraehoeQ.
kama tortoroeQ :	lampez o Sopat hazaQ ka kanoQ kanoQ SinraehoeQ?
kama kiSkaHat :	hayzaQ ka SaypapaHaS, QamiS, SaySiyat, bonong, poyomaQ, rokay, paywan, taHoH, Sow, Saw, kabalan, taroko, Sakiraya, Saytik.
kamatortoroeQ :	haynoQ QaehaeQ SinraehoeQ habaHan ka maeQiyaeh?
kamakiSkaHat :	QamiS habaHan ka maeQiyaeh.
kamatortoroeQ :	Qima QititiQan maeQiyaeh kanoQ SinraehoeQ?
kamakiSkaHat :	Sakiraya QititiQan ka maeQiyaeh.
kamatortoroeQ :	Qima mowahil ka kakrowaSekan kanoQ SinraehoeQ?
kama kiSkaHat :	SaypapaHaS ray taywan kaknamiSan, ila haHoQ wazwaz maQ hayzaQ ka maeQiyaeh.
